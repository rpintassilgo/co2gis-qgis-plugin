about=LeastCostPipeline combines raster-based least-cost path (LCP) routing with cell-level capital-cost (CAPEX) estimation for planning CO2 transport pipelines, in a single interactive QGIS interface. It implements the COMET multi-criteria cost surface model (Van den Broek et al., 2013), least-cost path computation via GRASS GIS, and CAPEX estimation with a pipeline diameter derived from the Darcy-Weisbach equation and booster stations on long segments. Two estimation modes (precise cell-by-cell and fast point-sampling) offer a user-controlled accuracy/speed trade-off. Requires the GRASS provider bundled with QGIS. Developed as the practical component of a Master's dissertation (ESTG / Polytechnic of Leiria) with the Net4CO2 collaborative laboratory.
